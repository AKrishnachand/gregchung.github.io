    2016

  • October 21: The group was awarded a 3-year funding (~$43,000 per year) from National Research Foundation of Korea through 기본연구지원사업 (2016R1D1A1B039934484).


  • October 14: A paper entitled "in silico discovery of metal-organic frameworks for precombustion CO2 capture using genetic algorithm", is published in Science Advances! Prof. J. Fraser Stoddart, who was awarded the 2016 Nobel Prize in Chemistry, was also part of the team in making this paper possible.


  • September 14: A paper entitled "High-throughput screening of MOFs for CO2 capture in the presence of water," is published in Langmuir.


  • April 28: Yongchul gives an invited talk at the spring Korean Institute of Chemical Engineers (KIChE) meeting at BEXCO in Busan, Korea.


  • March 2: The group was found at Pusan National University in Busan, Korea (South).


  • Jan 21: Yongchul visits the Chemical Engineering department at Case Western Reserve University.
